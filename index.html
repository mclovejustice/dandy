⚠️ Twisted Danger Tiers
  • Buy a Tape on even floors — any cheap one keeps Twisted Dandy away.
  • Failed skill check? Move away fast — Twisteds charge to your machine.
  • Team up on machines — multiple players complete extraction much faster.
  • Use Vee's Mic Check before starting a machine run to see all Twisteds.
  • High stealth toons can walk right past Twisteds without being spotted!
  • Spring Fever (Easter 2026) — +50% speed but Twisted Bassie throws vines; Cocoa hops obstacles!
💎 Earn Ichor Fast
  • Complete machines — 1 Ichor per 20% contributed (max 5 per machine).
  • Survive deep floors — Ichor scales with floor depth.
  • Encounter Twisteds — each unique encounter = 1 Ichor.
  • Use active ability once per floor for 5 bonus Ichor.
  • Easter 2026: earn Baskets from floors, daily logins, Lobby Egg Hunt & Dandy's elevator shop. Need ~10,300 for full completion!
