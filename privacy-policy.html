Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Dandy's World HQ is a free, fan-made companion app for the Roblox game Dandy's World. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Roblox Corporation or the official Dandy's World development team.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. This app does not require an account, login, or any personal information to use.

Local Storage

The app stores your preferences and progress (such as unlocked toons, team selections, and game scores) locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The app loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). Google's own privacy policy applies to this request. No other third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks are used.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. No personal information is collected from any user regardless of age.

Intellectual Property

Dandy's World is a game created by its respective owners. This app is an unofficial fan project. All game-related names, characters, and concepts belong to their respective copyright holders.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can raise an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
